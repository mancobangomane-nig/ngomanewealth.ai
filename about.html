Who We Are

Ngomane Investment Group (Pty) Ltd (“NIG”) is a hybrid SME consulting, compliance, automation, and AI & risk advisory firm that integrates strategy, governance, and digital systems into one unified service model.

The business exists to solve the most critical challenges facing South African SMEs: regulatory compliance, operational inefficiency, lack of structured strategy, weak management systems, and uncertainty around the responsible use of automation and artificial intelligence.

NIG focuses on business intelligence, systems, and execution, rather than basic digital production.
